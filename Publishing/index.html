Who we are

GMG = Great Minds Gathered

GMG Publication is not just a name – it's a movement. We're a small, independent publishing house with big dreams and a bigger heart.
From humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted name in the world of anthology publishing. Our goal? To spotlight new voices and create books that echo with meaning.
So far, we’ve published powerful anthologies that feature young, passionate writers from all walks of life — and we’re just getting started.
